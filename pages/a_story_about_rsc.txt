-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[0x01] THE RUNE MONOPOLY
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If you started Classic late, you don't understand how insane rune items were.

Rune wasn't "gear."

Rune was a STATUS SYMBOL.

Because in early Classic there was basically one truth:

Only one person could smith rune. It was a small club, and you weren't in it.

And for a while, the only name that mattered was:

Bluerose13x.

She could smith rune.
Which meant rune gear wasn't a normal market item.

It was like trying to buy diamonds from the only person in town who owns the mine and also sets the prices and also decides if you deserve to even speak to them.

If you wanted rune:
    - you paid insane prices
    - you waited
    - you hoped the seller wasn't "busy"
    - and if you didn't have connections, you were basically out

So to a bunch of kids watching Varrock like it was Wall Street, there was one obvious conclusion:

        "If we can create another 99 smith, we can break the monopoly.
         And if we break the monopoly, we get rich AND become part of history."

So yeah. We formed a crew.

We used Jerunk's character as a runner.

AUTOMATION (Rawh the Factory)

Not some modern botnet, just "I figured out how to get bars and I'm using it."

FUNDING THE OPERATION (Spider Egg Hustle)
